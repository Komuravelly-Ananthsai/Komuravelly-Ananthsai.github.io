This project analyzes and addresses the challenges Apple Inc. faces with the battery performance of its iOS devices. The objective is to recommend strategic actions that Apple can take to improve user satisfaction and maintain its competitive advantage in the tech industry.

Sneak Street – Streetwear & Sneaker Culture Hub - Ecommerce project

Built with a clean, modern aesthetic, Sneak Street offers a user-friendly interface that showcases featured collections, exclusive drops, and curated fashion content.
